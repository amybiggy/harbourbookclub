Welcome!

Dear readers, Welcome to Harbour Book Club!
Are you looking for a welcoming book club where you can share your honest opinions about the books you read?
Then look no further!
We meet monthly and read all different genres, so come along, get comfy and grab your drink of choice!

Our Books

Our Last Read

Fairy Tale is a dark fantasy novel by American author Stephen King. The novel follows Charlie Reade, a 17-year-old who inherits keys to a hidden, otherworldly realm, and finds himself leading the battle between forces of good and evil.

Our Current Read

The Spanish Love Deception is a rom-com following Catalina Martín who desperately needs a date to her sister’s wedding, and Aaron Blackford—her tall, handsome, condescending colleague—who surprisingly offers to step in. This fake dating, enemies to lovers book will have you hooked!

Our Next Read

Alicia Berenson lived a seemingly perfect life until one day six years ago. When she shot her husband in the head five times. Since then she hasn't spoken a single word. It's time to find out why.
